Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery areas?
We deliver across all major cities in Sri Lanka including Colombo, Kandy, Galle, and surrounding areas. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM within Colombo district.
How do I place a custom bouquet order?
You can create a custom bouquet using our Custom Bouquet Builder on our website, or contact us directly via phone or email. Our expert florists will work with you to create the perfect arrangement.
What is your return/refund policy?
We guarantee 100% satisfaction. If you're not happy with your purchase, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We'll work to make it right with a replacement or full refund.
Can I schedule a delivery in advance?
Yes! You can schedule deliveries up to 30 days in advance. Simply select your preferred delivery date during checkout. We'll ensure your flowers arrive fresh on the specified date.
Do you offer corporate services?
Absolutely! We provide corporate flower arrangements, event decoration, and bulk orders for businesses. Contact us for special corporate pricing and customized solutions.
